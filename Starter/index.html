ABOUT US

Welcome to The Museum Of Candy, where massive sugar cotton wool structure merge with a mouthwatering taffy twistedness. Explore our diverse sanctuary of confectionery blissfulness and be transported to a creative wonderland with a very unique sensory experience.

HISTORY

The dessert museum is an interactive tasting adventure into 8 mouthwatering rooms that transforms your favorite sweets into one epic imaginative wonderland. Join our candy kingdom guiding guests through our sprawling sanctuary of confectionery bliss.

HIGHLIGHTS

  • Bewitched Bakery: Bake in our bewitched bakery where you can have your fortunes told by a big fortune cookie.
  • Room Of Neve Ending Bubbles: The Biggest Gumball Machine you have ever seen.
  • Banana Beach: Jump into a beach full of banana sprinkles and banana split slides and many more .
